Welcome to SGEMA

South Gujarat Event Manager Associa6on also known as “SGEMA” was founded in 2013 by the veteran of event industry of South Gujarat and is based in diamond and textile capital of the world, SURAT.

SGEMA was mainly formed to professionalize and channelize the South Gujarat Event Industry by providing a common plaform where all event managers and associate members of the region can network & collaborate thereby creating more opportunities & possibilities for self and others by using vast pool of knowledge, resources and experience each member possess thus minimizing the spirit of competition and harness the feeling of collaboration and togetherness.

SGEMA is a one stop window for any vendor to showcase and promote their products to the elite event managers who holds more than 80% market share of the entire South Gujarat region and are responsible for all the major events region has ever seen.

SGEMA is the only place for companies looking for opportunities to explore and enter the South Gujarat event market and boasts of having associate members based throughout INDIA and is also now open to welcome associate members globally.

SGEMA also helps in liaising with various government bodies and several other associations of the industry thereby facilitating in getting co-operation and knowledge upgradation from all the sectors for smooth running of events and event business.

SGEMA is also a way for all the members to give back to the society as and when required or asked for by helping people, organisations, associations and the society during the time of need and crisis.
